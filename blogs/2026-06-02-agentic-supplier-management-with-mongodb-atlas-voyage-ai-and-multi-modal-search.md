Retailers can modernize supplier management using MongoDB Atlas, Voyage AI, and multi-modal search to build resilience against supply chain disruptions, enabling real-time visibility, semantic discovery of alternative suppliers, and intelligent responsiveness to external shocks. MongoDB's flexible data model, vector search capabilities, and low-latency data access via Change Streams are positioned as essential tools for transforming supply chain management from reactive crisis handling into proactive, data-driven decision-making as legacy ERP systems become operational bottlenecks.
