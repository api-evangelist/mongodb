The Indian multinational online car marketplace Cars24 serves 300 million users globally. The company offers services that span sales, insurance, maintenance, financing, and more, reshaping the entire car ownership journey. Speaking at MongoDB .local Bengaluru in July 2025 , Pradeep Sharma, Head of Technology at Cars24, shared how MongoDB has been a key driver of Car24’s digital transformation journey.
