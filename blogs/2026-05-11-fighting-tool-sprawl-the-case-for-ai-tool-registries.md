As enterprise AI agent adoption scales, the absence of centralized, organization-level tool infrastructure is producing compounding costs. When adoption is built around optimizing for deployment speed, enterprises expose themselves to a combination of risks: duplicated engineering effort, security exposure, and operational opacity. Every enterprise needs its own shared tool registry, one that reflects its specific regulatory environment, security posture, and operational conventions.
